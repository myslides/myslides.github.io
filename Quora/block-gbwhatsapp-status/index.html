The Privacy Problem

When you delete a WhatsApp status, modified apps like GB WhatsApp keep showing it because they save statuses locally on devices. This guide provides the only working solution to completely remove your status from all clients.

The Verified Solution

Complete account deletion is required to remove status traces from all modified WhatsApp clients. This is the only method that works.

Important Considerations

  • This action permanently deletes all your chats, groups, and media
  • You'll need to rejoin groups after reactivating
  • Backup important conversations before proceeding
  • This only works for statuses deleted within the last 30 days

Tested and confirmed working on WhatsApp version 2.23.18.84 and GB WhatsApp 17.20

Complete Step-by-Step Guide

1

Delete Existing Status

First, remove any sensitive status from your account:

  1. Open WhatsApp
  2. Go to Status tab
  3. Tap on your status
  4. Select "Delete"
This alone won't remove it from GB WhatsApp
2

Initiate Account Deletion

Permanently delete your WhatsApp account:

  1. Tap ⋮ (Android) or ⚙️ (iOS)
  2. Go to Settings > Account
  3. Select "Delete my account"
3

Confirm Deletion

Complete the deletion process:

  1. Enter your phone number in international format
  2. Select "Privacy concerns" as reason
  3. Tap "DELETE MY ACCOUNT"
Warning: This cannot be undone! Backup important data first.
4

Reactivate Account

After 72 hours (critical waiting period):

  1. Reinstall WhatsApp
  2. Verify your phone number
  3. Set up your account
Your deleted status will now be completely gone from all clients

Expert Tips

Best Timing

Perform this when your status was deleted within last 30 days for maximum effectiveness.

Communication

Inform close contacts you'll be temporarily unavailable during this process.

Security

Enable two-step verification after reactivating your account.

Prevention

Never leave your phone unlocked in public spaces to avoid tampering.
