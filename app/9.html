Discover Auli, Uttarakhand

Auli, located in Uttarakhand, is a beautiful hill station known for its ski slopes and panoramic mountain views. It is a popular destination for adventure enthusiasts and those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. The serene environment, combined with opportunities for skiing, trekking, and sightseeing, makes Auli a must-visit location.
