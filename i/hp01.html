6 Nights / 7 Days Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Local Exploration

  • Breakfast: Welcome drink and snacks at the hotel
  • Morning Activity: Check-in, rest, and explore the local area
  • Lunch: Local street food tasting (momos, thukpa)
  • Afternoon Activity: Visit nearby markets and temples
  • Games: Board games and family time in the evening

Day 2: City Tour & Sightseeing

  • Breakfast: Traditional paratha with curd
  • Morning Activity: Visit Jakhoo Temple
  • Lunch: Local cuisine at a restaurant
  • Afternoon Activity: Explore the Viceregal Lodge and take a toy train ride
  • Games: Mini-golf or bowling in the evening

Day 3: Adventure Activities

  • Breakfast: Buffet breakfast at the hotel
  • Morning Activity: Paragliding at Billing
  • Lunch: Picnic lunch in the mountains
  • Afternoon Activity: Trekking to Triund
  • Games: Campfire games in the evening

Day 4: Cultural Exploration

  • Breakfast: Continental breakfast
  • Morning Activity: Visit local handicraft markets
  • Lunch: Traditional Himachali cuisine
  • Afternoon Activity: Attend a local dance or music performance
  • Games: Family trivia night

Day 5: Nature and Wildlife

  • Breakfast: Healthy breakfast at the hotel
  • Morning Activity: Visit a local wildlife sanctuary
  • Lunch: Packed lunch in nature
  • Afternoon Activity: Nature walk and photography session
  • Games: Nature scavenger hunt

Day 6: Relaxation and Leisure

  • Breakfast: Breakfast buffet
  • Morning Activity: Spa and wellness session
  • Lunch: Lunch at the hotel restaurant
  • Afternoon Activity: Free time for shopping and relaxation
  • Games: Card games or movie marathon

Day 7: Departure

  • Breakfast: Farewell breakfast at the hotel
  • Morning Activity: Last-minute shopping and sightseeing
  • Lunch: Lunch at a popular local restaurant
  • Departure: Check-out and transfer to the airport
