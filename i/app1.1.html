1. Asia

    Visit Thailand's temples, trek in the Himalayas, enjoy cherry blossoms in Japan, or island-hop in the Philippines.
  2. Europe

    Tour historic cities like Rome and Paris, ski the Alps, cruise the Greek Islands, or explore Iceland’s natural wonders.
  3. North America

    Visit national parks like Yellowstone, enjoy beach vacations in the Caribbean, explore New York City, or road-trip across Canada.
  4. South America

    See the Amazon Rainforest, hike to Machu Picchu, explore Patagonia, or relax on Brazil's famous beaches.
  5. Africa

    Go on a safari in Kenya, explore the deserts of Morocco, hike Mount Kilimanjaro, or relax on Zanzibar's beaches.
  6. Australia (Oceania)

    Dive the Great Barrier Reef, explore New Zealand’s landscapes, relax on Fiji's beaches, or visit Sydney’s iconic landmarks.
