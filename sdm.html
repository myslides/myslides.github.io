Himachal Pradesh Winter Itinerary (January 2025)

This itinerary is designed for a self-drive trip starting on January 26th, 2025, keeping in mind winter conditions and road closures.

Day 1 (January 26th - Republic Day): Arrival in Chandigarh & Drive to Shimla

Travel time: ~3-4 hours

  • Arrive at Chandigarh Airport (IXC).
  • Pick up your pre-booked rental car. Ensure you have all necessary documents.
  • Start your drive to Shimla (via NH5). Be aware of potential traffic delays due to Republic Day.
  • Check into your hotel in Shimla (with parking).
  • Evening: Relax. Many places might have limited hours due to the holiday.

Day 2: Shimla Sightseeing

  • Morning: Visit Jakhoo Temple (drive partway and then walk or take a pony).
  • Afternoon: Explore Shimla town:
    • Walk on the Mall Road and Ridge.
    • Visit Christ Church.
    • Visit the Vice Regal Lodge (Indian Institute of Advanced Study).
  • Alternative for Kufri (If road closed): Visit nearby areas like Fagu or Naldehra (if accessible), or spend more time exploring Shimla.

Day 3: Drive to Dharamshala

Travel time: ~7-8 hours

  • Morning: Visit the State Museum or local markets (if open).
  • Start your drive to Dharamshala.
  • Possible Stop: Palampur tea gardens.
  • Check into your hotel in McleodGanj (with parking).
  • Evening: Explore the McleodGanj market.

Day 4: Dharamshala & McleodGanj

  • Morning: Visit the Tsuglagkhang Complex (Dalai Lama Temple).
  • Afternoon: Visit the Namgyal Monastery and Tibetan Museum.
  • Late Afternoon: Visit Bhagsu Waterfall and Bhagsunath Temple.

Day 5: Drive to Manali

Travel time: ~6-7 hours

  • Morning: Visit St. John in the Wilderness Church.
  • Start your drive to Manali. Check road conditions before starting.
  • Possible Stops: Baijnath Temple, Kullu Shawl Factories.
  • Check into your hotel in Manali.

Day 6: Manali Sightseeing (Limited Options Due to Winter)

  • Visit Hadimba Temple.
  • Explore Old Manali.
  • Visit Vashisht village and hot springs.
  • Visit the Manali market.
  • Avoid Planning for Rohtang Pass: It will almost certainly be closed.

Day 7: Manali & Drive Towards Chandigarh

  • Morning: Enjoy the winter scenery in Manali.
  • Start your drive back towards Chandigarh. Consider an overnight stay in Mandi or Sundar Nagar.

Day 8: Drive to Chandigarh & Departure

  • Continue your drive to Chandigarh.
  • Return your rental car at Chandigarh Airport.
  • Depart from Chandigarh.

Important Considerations for a Winter Self-Drive Trip:

  • Road Conditions: Mountain roads can be challenging in winter. Be a confident driver and drive cautiously. Check road conditions frequently.
  • Driving Times: Driving times can be longer than expected.
  • Parking: Parking can be limited.
  • Altitude Sickness: Be prepared for altitude changes.
  • Winter Clothing: Pack extremely warm clothing.
  • Republic Day: Be aware of potential closures or limited hours on January 26th.
